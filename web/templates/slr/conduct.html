Manual Workflow (Current)

While the automated tools are being developed, you can manually conduct your systematic review using the protocol you've created. Here's a suggested workflow:

1. Execute Database Searches

Use your search strings to query each database in your protocol. Document results in a spreadsheet.

2. Screen and Select Studies

Apply inclusion/exclusion criteria to titles, abstracts, then full texts. Track decisions and reasons for exclusion.

3. Assess Study Quality

Use your quality assessment checklist to evaluate each included study.

4. Extract Data

Systematically extract the data fields specified in your protocol.
